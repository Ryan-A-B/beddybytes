## About BeddyBytes
BeddyBytes is a privacy first baby monitor which streams all video/audio over the local network using WebRTC. WebRTC is configured to work without STUN or TURN so anything outside of the local network will fail, this is deliberate. BeddyBytes uses devices people already own potentially giving them a new breath of life. There are 2 roles devices can take inside BeddyBytes, baby station and parent station. Both roles require a modern browser (Websockets + WebRTC), additionally the baby station requires a microphone and optionally a camera.
